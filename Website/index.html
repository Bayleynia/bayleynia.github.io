Need to know things about Volleyball

Volleyball is a big fall sport that many love, I love to play on my team and just for fun too.It is a relatively easy sport to learn, and due to that you can get into to it at any point in your life. While you can just play it for fun, many love to play competitively. Usally when you play on a Volleyball team, or any team, there are different ranks depending on two things. Your skill level and experience.

A Volleyball team consists of 12 players, with six being on the court at one time.

  1. Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan, physical director of the Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA) in Holyoke, Massachusetts.
  2. Volleyball did not become an Olympic sport until 1964.
  3. Volleyball was designed as an indoor sport for businessmen who found the new game of basketball too vigorous.
